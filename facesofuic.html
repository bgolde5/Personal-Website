About

This project is called "Faces of UIC". This was a brief summer project that I did for the University of Illinois at Chicago Admissions Office.

Main Prerequisits

Working knowledge of HTML, CSS, Photoshop, and Photography. People skills is also a must. :)

Description

Faces of UIC is similar to that of the popular site "Humans of New York" (if you haven't heard of the site, google it now and you'll find yourself pretty enertained). I did this project because I like meeting new people, I like photography, and I like playing with websites. This was a great combination of the three.

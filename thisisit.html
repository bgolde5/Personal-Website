About

This is It is a meetup.com group that and friend and I have co-founded. You can see the description below from the perspective of someone browsing meetup.com or you can click the image above to see the group in action.

Description

THIS IS IT is all about creating life long friendships, having fun with peers, and winning at life. In this group you will have the opportunity to be actively supported, challenged to excel in your career, encouraged to strengthen and build new relationships, motivated to achieve your personal goals, and more. We are a group of young men, ages 18-35ish, who cheer each other on and don't take each other's bullshit at the same time. In this group we truly look out for each other and want to do the same with you. If you're interested, we welcome young men from all walks of life.
